Wills, Trusts & Estate Planning

Married Couples: Even with joint property, you STILL need a Will and Trust. Without a Will, the state decides who gets your assets and raises your children.

Are you okay with the state making these decisions?

  • What happens to the surviving spouse's estate?
  • Who's the guardian of your kids?
  • What about non-joint assets, jewelry, car?

Benefits of Proper Planning

  • Trusts avoid probate, protect privacy, and control distribution timing
  • Estate tax strategies keep more with your family
  • Healthcare directives ensure YOUR wishes are honored
